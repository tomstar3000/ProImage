Pro Spotlight

This month we feature Jensen Sutta Photography. Jensen shoots weddings, events and non-profit photography. He recalls being on a road trip in college when the 16 passenger van rolled 6 times. During his year-long recovery, Jensen came to the realization that life is too short not to do what he loves.

To improve his shooting and sales, Jensen has taken an approach similar to Austrailian weddings. He encourages the bride and groom during the planning stages to incorporate more time, i.e. to have the cermony in the afternoon and the reception in the evening. "This extra time allows for more unique, playful and sellable final images."
Jensen manages his business in Santa Barbara, CA and Denver, CO and shoots all over the world. You can see his work at www.jensensutta.com
